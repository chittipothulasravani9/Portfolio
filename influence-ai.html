The Influence of AI on E-Governance and Cyber Security in Smart Cities

This project explores the integration of Artificial Intelligence into E-Governance frameworks within Smart Cities, focusing on enhancing decision-making efficiency, automating administrative tasks, and ensuring robust cyber security measures. It investigates how AI-powered analytics can predict and prevent security breaches, safeguard citizen data, and enable transparent governance. The study also covers the role of IoT in urban infrastructure, highlighting the need for adaptive and intelligent cyber defense systems to secure interconnected devices and networks in modern smart cities.
